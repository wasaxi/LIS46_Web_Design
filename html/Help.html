This “help” page will guide you to use our JAFFEE website.
Scene1: Landing Page

Notice: Our website requires that you must register as member first.

If you haven’t been member, click the “register” button to register first.

If you have been member, click the “Log in” button to log in directly.

Scene2: Register

Type in your basic information.

And our website provide different learning course for user to start. Please choose your learning experience and beginning chapter based on your actual learning situation.

Scene 3: Log in

Type in your Account name and Password.

If you confirm the information, you can click the “Log in” to continue.

If you haven’t been member, you can click the “Register” button to jump into register page.

Scene 4: Home Page

This is the home page of JAFFEE website.

From here, you can have a brief acknowledgment of JAFFEE websites’ s function. And from the navigation bar on the top or by clicking the logo, you can move to other pages easily.

Scene 5: COURSE page

There is a catalogue on the left, you can follow it to move into different courses by clicking according title.

In every chapter, there are brief introduction of knowledge points in this LECTURE, VIDEO for better understanding , and QUIZ for you to test.

Scene 6: TEST directory page

This is the summary of your tests in process.

And you can see the difficulty, average score of all students, your score and your learning progress under the corresponding title.

Scene 7: Test page

In test page, there are various kinds of questions, such as multiple choice, judgment questions, and fill-in questions.

Scene 8：USER CENTRE

In this page, you can see your account’s basic information and learning records such as study progress and learning statistics.

After you have finished all the lectures and pass the test, you will have the opportunity to get your certificate.

Scene 9: certificate page

In the end, if you still have any questions about website use, you can contact with us following the page footing.
